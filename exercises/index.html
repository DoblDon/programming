Introduction

This is the practical task assignment for the course Interaction Programming. The block course is taught at the Institute of Ergonomics of the Technische Universität München in the context of master courses in Human Factors Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Computer Sciences in the . This document is authored by Markus Zimmermann, David Schopf, and Niklas Lütteken.

The exercises aim to learn the web technologies HTML5/CSS3/JavaScript by developing a weather application during the course.
